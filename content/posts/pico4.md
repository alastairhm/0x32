After tweaking it slightly from the [source](http://warp.povusers.org/MandScripts/python.html) it ended up like this [mandelbrot.py](https://github.com/alastairhm/pico_w/blob/main/mandelbrot.py)

It does not process very fast in MicroPython as can be seen from [this video](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/dqHLxU5frKw) and would probably would look better on a single colour OLED. 

I am going to try doing the same in C to see how much of a speed improvement there is over MicroPython.
